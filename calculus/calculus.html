Calculus Resources Page

Since there are limited online resources for Calculus / Maths standards, here are some of the key sites which I think can provide lots of help, especially if you look through them for what you specifically need

  • All NZQA Math Standards

    • Level 1
    • Level 2
    • Level 3

    All available NZQA Math standards with Past Papers, Marking Schemes, and Exemplars for levels 1, 2, and 3


  • Infinityplusone Youtube Channel

    • All Standards Playlists

    Brilliant concept explanations and past paper walk throughs specifically for NZQA. This links to all his playlists for every level / topic


  • Studytime Math Pages

    • Level 1
    • Level 2
    • Level 3

    Links to each level of maths on studytime. Contains walkthrough guides, strategy videos, and subject checklists


  • Khan Academy

    • Trigonometry
    • Precalc
    • Calculus
    • Algebra

    Short, helpful explanatory videos on a huge variety of topics, not just maths. Here are some fundamental topics but feel free to use the search bar to find more specific resources to help you.
